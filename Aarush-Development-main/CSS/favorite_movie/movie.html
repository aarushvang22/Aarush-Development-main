Kalki 2898 AD

In a post-apocalyptic world, a group of people set out to save a woman pregnant with Lord Vishnu's 10th and final avatar, Kalki, from an evil supreme god-king, Yaskin.

Cast

  • Prabhas - Bhairava
  • Amitabh Bachchan - Ashwatthama
  • Kamal Haasan - Supreme Yaskin
  • Saswata Chatterjee - Commander Manas
  • Anil George - Counsellor Bani
